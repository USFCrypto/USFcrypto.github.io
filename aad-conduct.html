Workshop environment

Applied Algebra Days is committed to fostering a positive and professional environment for all participants. We expect attendees to interact respectfully and to contribute to a workshop culture that is welcoming across career stage, background, and research area.

Expected conduct

Participants are expected to behave professionally and respectfully throughout the event, including during talks, meals, social activities, and informal discussions.

External policies

For this conference we are adopting the Policy Against Harassment developed by the Association for Computing Machinery. Participants in the Florida Applied Algebra Days are expected to adhere to all of the policies described at that link.

Reporting concerns

If you experience or observe conduct inconsistent with this policy, please contact one of the organizers as soon as possible. Concerns will be handled seriously and respectfully.
