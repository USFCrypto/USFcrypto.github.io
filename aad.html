About the series

Workshops that connect research and community

Applied Algebra Days is part of the broader RTG ecosystem in Applied Algebra at USF. Each edition brings together faculty, postdoctoral scholars, graduate students, and external participants for focused days of talks, discussion, and community-building.

Research-focused

Programs are built around active mathematical directions in cryptography, coding theory, computational algebra, and quantum information.

Community-oriented

The workshops are designed not only to showcase research, but also to help build a durable Applied Algebra community at USF and beyond.

Trainee-friendly

Students and postdocs benefit from exposure to current topics, leading researchers, and a welcoming workshop environment connected to the RTG.
