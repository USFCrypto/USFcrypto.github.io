Research at the USF Crypto Center

This page shows the quieter academic style for inner pages: simple headings, readable text, and a few visual anchors.

Post-Quantum Cryptography

Research topics include lattices, coding theory, isogeny-based cryptography, and rigorous security foundations for cryptographic systems expected to remain secure in the presence of quantum computers.

Applied Cryptography

Work in applied cryptography spans protocol design, secure systems, implementation issues, and the bridge between theoretical cryptography and practice.

Hardware and Embedded Security

The center also connects with systems and hardware security, including embedded cryptography and implementation-aware defenses.
